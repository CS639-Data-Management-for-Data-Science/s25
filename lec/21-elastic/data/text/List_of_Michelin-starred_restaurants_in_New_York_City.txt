{{Short description|List of restaurants in New York City with a Michelin-star rating}}

[[File:Landscape Shot of Eleven Madison Park (9284511991).jpg|thumb|Eleven Madison Park, a multi-year 3-star Michelin restaurant]]
This article contains a complete '''list of Michelin-starred restaurants in New York City''' and [[Westchester County, New York|Westchester County]]. The 2006 edition was the first edition of the ''[[Michelin Guide]] to [[New York City]]'' to be published. It was the first time that Michelin published a ''Red Guide'' for a region outside Europe.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2005/02/23/dining/the-tire-man-eats-new-york.html|last=Fabricant|first=Florence|author-link=Florence Fabricant|title=The Tire Man Eats New York|date=23 February 2005|work=[[The New York Times]]|access-date=3 July 2015|archive-date=29 May 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150529193038/http://www.nytimes.com/2005/02/23/dining/the-tire-man-eats-new-york.html|url-status=live}}</ref> In the 2020 edition, the Guide began to include restaurants outside the city's five boroughs, adding Westchester County restaurants to its listing.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://guide.michelin.com/us/en/new-york-state/article/news-and-views/michelin-guide-nyc-2020-westchester-county-expansion|title=MICHELIN Guide NYC Expands to Westchester County|date=2019-10-11|website=MICHELIN The 2021 guide was delayed due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]],<ref>{{cite web |last1=Pomranz |first1=Mike |title=America's 2021 Michelin Guides Will All Be Delayed Due to COVID-19 |url=https://www.foodandwine.com/news/michelin-2021-us-guides-delayed-covid-19 |website=Food and Wine Magazine |access-date=22 October 2020 |archive-date=23 October 2020 but finally was released on May 6, 2021. A new award — the green star, for sustainable gastronomy — was given to [[Blue Hill at Stone Barns]], which has two Michelin stars.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Fabricant|first=Florence|date=2021-05-06|title=Michelin Announces Stars for New York Restaurants|language=en-US|work=The New York Times|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2021/05/06/dining/michelin-stars-new-york.html|access-date=2021-05-09|issn=0362-4331|archive-date=2023-07-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230731213110/https://www.nytimes.com/2021/05/06/dining/michelin-stars-new-york.html|url-status=live}}</ref> Despite the temporary closure of three-star [[Eleven Madison Park]] due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] and its pivot to a vegan menu upon reopening in June 2021, the restaurant has retained its three-star rating in the latest guides.
