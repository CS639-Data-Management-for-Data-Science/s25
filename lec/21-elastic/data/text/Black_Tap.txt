'''Black Tap Craft Burgers & Beer''' is an international gourmet burger restaurant chain run by restaurateur Chris Barish.  The name Black Tap is a reference to Craft Beers where Black Tap handles are a mainstay.<ref>{{cite web|last=Lalley |first=Heather |url=https://www.restaurantbusinessonline.com/emerging-brands/how-black-tap-becoming-international-brand |title=How Black Tap is becoming an international brand |publisher=Restaurantbusinessonline.com |date=2019-07-11 |accessdate=2021-02-20}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web |title=Chris Barish Brings The Bar-ish NYC Burger Joint, Black Tap, To Singapore |url=https://www.prestigeonline.com/sg/lifestyle/wine-plus-dine/chris-barish-brings-bar-ish-nyc-burger-joint-black-tap-singapore/}}</ref>

Black Tap was founded in 2015 as a 15-seater in [[New York City]] by restaurateur Chris Barish, son of [[Planet Hollywood]] founder and film producer [[Keith Barish]].

The restaurant was noted for its creatively-decorated milkshakes,<ref>{{cite web |last=Casey |first=Nell |date=2016-12-27 |url=http://gothamist.com/2016/12/27/black_tap_dumbo_les.php |title=Black Tap is Bringing Its Insane Milkshakes to DUMBO |website=Gothamist |access-date=2017-01-03 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170111162102/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/27/black_tap_dumbo_les.php |archive-date=2017-01-11 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://gothamist.com/2016/01/27/black_tap_milkshake_insanity.php|title=Video: Meet The Milkshake That Broke The Internet... And Our Intern|work=Gothamist|first=Nell|last=Casey|date=27 January 2016|access-date=5 April 2017|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170206194342/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/27/black_tap_milkshake_insanity.php|archive-date=6 February 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.news.com.au/lifestyle/food/restaurants-bars/americas-most-instagrammed-restaurant-youve-never-heard-of/news-story/688ca6d10d28f708054b23be8477b4cd |title=Sugar Factory: America's most Instagrammed restaurant is in New York City |publisher=News.com.au |last=Willis |first=Charlotte |date=2016-12-08 |accessdate=2021-02-20}}</ref> In 2016, Black Tap received ''Time Out'' magazine's People's Choice award for 'Best Burger'.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Carney|first=Abby|date=2016-08-19|title=And the winner of Battle of the Burger is…|url=https://www.timeout.com/newyork/blog/and-the-winner-of-battle-of-the-burger-is-081916|access-date=2021-02-20|website=Time Out New York|language=en-US}}</ref>

Black Tap owns locations in [[New York City]]'s [[Midtown Manhattan|Midtown]], [[SoHo, Manhattan|Soho]], and [[Herald Square]] neighborhoods, [[Las Vegas]], [[Anaheim, California|Anaheim]], [[Singapore]], [[Dallas]], <ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-08-10 |title=Inside Texas' first Black Tap, serving burgers and CrazyShakes near the AAC |url=https://www.dallasnews.com/food/restaurant-news/2023/08/10/inside-texas-first-black-tap-serving-burgers-and-crazyshakes-near-the-aac/ |access-date=2023-08-17 |website=Dallas News |language=en}}</ref> Geneva, Zürich, [[Dubai]], [[Kuwait]], [[Abu Dhabi]], [[Bahrain]], [[Erbil]], and [[Saudi Arabia]].

In May 2023, Black Tap opened a new location in Nashville and won Nashville Scene's Best Burger award. <ref>{{Cite web |last=Labrecque |first=Samantha |date=2023-05-02 |title=Black Tap Craft Burgers & Beer is Opening in Nashville |url=https://nashvillelifestyles.com/api/content/ac7071c8-d873-11ec-aa12-12274efc5439/ |access-date=2023-08-17 |website=Nashville Lifestyles |language=en-us}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-07-18 |title=Black Tap Nashville Tennessee Burger |url=https://www.nashvillescene.com/black-tap-nashville-tennessee-burger/image_6ca8c616-2580-11ee-81df-7b168eca7361.html |access-date=2023-08-17 |website=Nashville Scene |language=en}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-05-21 |title=Black Tap Craft Burgers & Beer opens first Southern location in Nashville |url=https://www.wkrn.com/news/local-news/nashville/black-tap-craft-burgers-beer-opens-first-southern-location-in-nashville/ |access-date=2023-08-17 |website=WKRN News 2 |language=en-US}}</ref>

Black Tap is known for their unique milkshakes called "Crazyshakes®" which Black Tap owns the trademark for. Flavors include Cookies 'N Cream Supreme, Strawberry Shortcake, The Cakeshake®  among other locally crafted shakes for each local market. <ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-08-10 |title=Inside Texas' first Black Tap, serving burgers and CrazyShakes near the AAC |url=https://www.dallasnews.com/food/restaurant-news/2023/08/10/inside-texas-first-black-tap-serving-burgers-and-crazyshakes-near-the-aac/ |access-date=2023-08-21 |website=Dallas News |language=en}}</ref>

Celebrities have frequently been seen endorsing Black Tap Burgers including [[The Weeknd]], [[Joe Jonas]], [[Kate Hudson]], and [[Katie Holmes]]. <ref>{{Cite web |last=Krein |first=Debra |date=2017-07-19 |title=Celebrities' NYC Guilty Pleasure – Black Tap Craft Burgers & Beer |url=https://hollywoodlife.com/2017/07/19/celebs-black-tap-nyc-shakes-burgers-pics/ |access-date=2023-08-31 |website=Hollywood Life |language=en-US}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web |last=courtneyb |date=2023-06-26 |title=Celeb Hotspot Black Tap NYC Hosts Epic CrazyShake Birthday Party For 7-Year Old CEO - The Source |url=https://thesource.com/2023/06/26/celeb-hotspot-black-tap-nyc-hosts-epic-crazyshake-birthday-party-for-7-year-old-ceo/,%20https://thesource.com/2023/06/26/celeb-hotspot-black-tap-nyc-hosts-epic-crazyshake-birthday-party-for-7-year-old-ceo/ |access-date=2023-08-31 |language=en-US}}</ref>

== International Expansion ==
In 2018, Black Tap opened in Singapore. In order to maintain strict quality control standards, they continued to import US beef for its burgers and import candies from the United States.<ref>{{Cite web |title= |url=https://www.prestigeonline.com/sg/lifestyle/wine-plus-dine/chris-barish-brings-bar-ish-nyc-burger-joint-black-tap-singapore/}}</ref>
