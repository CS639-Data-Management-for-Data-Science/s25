'''Just Salad''' is a chain of [[fast casual restaurant]]s<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |last=nicole |title=Just Salad Opens first Chicago Location |url=https://www.qsrmagazine.com/news/just-salad-opens-first-chicago-location |access-date=2016-02-02 |website=www.qsrmagazine.com|date=10 April 2015 }}</ref><ref>[https://www.usmagazine.com/food/news/daphne-oz-partners-with-just-salad-to-create-golden-glow-salad/ "Daphne Oz Partners With Just Salad to Create Dish That Lets You ‘Glow From Within’"] ''Us Magazine''. == History ==
The company was founded in 2006 by Nick Kenner and Rob Crespi.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Dude Food: An Interview With Nick Kenner of Just Salad - Big Girls Small Kitchen|url = http://www.biggirlssmallkitchen.com/2011/01/dude-food-an-interview-with-nick-kenner-of-just-salad.html|website = Big Girls Small Kitchen|access-date = 2016-02-02|language = en-US}}</ref>

The first location opened at 320 Park Avenue in [[New York City]] in 2006. Today, the company operates 70 store locations, primarily in the United States. <ref>[https://www.forbes.com/sites/aliciakelso/2019/09/12/another-win-for-the-plant-based-movement-just-salad-replaces-steak-with-beyond-meatballs/#5290530aeddb "Another Win For The Plant-Based Movement: Just Salad Replaces Steak With Beyond Meatballs"] ''Forbes''.
